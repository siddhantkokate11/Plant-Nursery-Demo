Our Story

A Family's Passion
for Greenery

GreenVista started in 2008 as a small family-run nursery in Pune with one simple belief — every home, every office, every corner of the city deserves a touch of nature. What began in a modest plot has grown into one of Pune's most trusted plant nurseries, with over 200 plant varieties and a team that genuinely loves what they do.

We're not just selling plants. We guide you, we follow up, we show up when your plant needs re-potting help at 6 PM on a weekday. That's how local business works — with real relationships. Our team speaks your language, understands Pune's climate, and picks the right plant for your exact space.
